Blog of a home crypto miner 2 months post Ethereum mining

After Ethereum went proof of stake most of the mining profits dried up as Ethereum was paying out a vast majority of the daily payments to miners.

At the height of the mining market I was renting out my Ethereum and Monero hash power for Bitcoin and earning over $30 a day with 3 computers.
The GPUs were mining Ethereum and the CPUs were mining Monero. Later I brought on an older GPU with which I rented out Ravencoin hash power.

Along the way I added 3 Application Specific Integrated Circuits (ASICs) for mining. Then I brought the old GPU offline along with another older GPU and added a new GPU and 3 new computers. This modernized my farm and I am now running some of the latest hardware.

I am currently mining Neoxa on my GPUs, Monero on my CPUs, I have 1 ASIC mining Grin, and 2 ASICs mining Ethereum Classic.
I developed a website to to monitor my mining stats in real time, BlastMining.net.
